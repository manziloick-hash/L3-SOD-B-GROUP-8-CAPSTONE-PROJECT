Our Story

Born in Kimihurura,
inspired by two continents

The Beginning

A passion for craft,
a home in Kigali

Pâtisserie Royale was founded by pastry artisans who trained in France and fell in love with Kigali. We opened our doors on KG 7 Avenue to bring the discipline of French pâtisserie together with the warmth and hospitality of Rwanda.

Everything is made by hand, every morning. We source coffee from local cooperatives, use seasonal Rwandan fruits, and pair them with French technique to create something genuinely unique to Kimihurura.

What We Stand For

Our values

🌿

Local first

We source coffee, fruits, and ingredients from Rwandan farmers and cooperatives whenever possible.

👐

Always handmade

Every pastry, cake, and bread is assembled by hand. No machines cut corners on our craft.

⭐

Uncompromising quality

We use French butter, Californian nuts, and ceremonial-grade matcha. Quality is non-negotiable.
